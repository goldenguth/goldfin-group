Most business owners assume their company has a set value. It doesn't. Your business is worth exactly what a qualified buyer will pay for it, and understanding what drives that number before you go to market is one of the most valuable things you can do.

## The three approaches

Professionals use three approaches to value a business: the asset approach, the income approach, and the market approach. For most service-oriented businesses, the income approach and its multiple of earnings is what matters most. Everyone fixates on the profit number, but the multiple is where the real leverage lives.
