Those who dream of a significant liquidity event treat it like the finish line. It's just the beginning. After you sell, every opportunity suddenly comes in quotation marks.

## The 5R Filter

Most decks sell the upside. Your filter should help you find the downside. The five Rs are Reason, Role, Risk, Return path, and Relationship. If two or three of them don't pass, move on. It's how every post-exit opportunity gets pressure-tested before it touches your capital.
