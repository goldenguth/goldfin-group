Most business owners consider selling too late. And by the time they realize it, they've often missed the most valuable window in their financial life.

## Swing Theory

When I was a kid, I learned one rule about jumping off swings the hard way. Jump on the way up, and you fly. Jump on the way down, and you eat wood chips. Selling a business works the same way.

Buyers don't pay for what your business is today. They pay for where it's going. When you sell on the upswing, with strong numbers and a believable growth story, you're confident, selective, and you don't need the deal. Buyers feel that, and it changes everything about your leverage.
