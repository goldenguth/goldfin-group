Most of the business owners I talk to don't have a money problem. They have a decision problem.

## Indecision is more expensive than a bad decision

A bad decision can usually be corrected. But indecision tends to linger for years. And during those years, opportunities disappear, tax strategies expire, and families are left without clarity. That's the real issue.
