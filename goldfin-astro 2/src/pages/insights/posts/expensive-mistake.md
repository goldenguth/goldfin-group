The deal is done. You're independently wealthy. And quietly, somewhere in the back of your mind, you're thinking: now what?

## The Goldfin Moment

The wire hitting the account isn't the finish line. The owners who transition best are the ones who transitioned into something meaningful, instead of simply transitioning out of something profitable. That's what we call the Goldfin Moment: the day you realize you hit your financial goal and wake up with a clear sense of purpose.
